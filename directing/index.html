As an actor/director I believe that the our role as artists is first and foremost to story tell. Towards this goal, I aim to create highly collaborative rehearsal environments which provide common ground for innovation, creativity, and discovery.

My training as an actor and experience working with other actors has highly influenced my directing style. I believe in critical and probing script analysis as a foundation for any production. With this base, I work to develop the instincts of each actor and empower them to articulate their choices, intentions, and needs. I frequently uses viewpoints, and clowning techniques to create a common language in the rehearsal room. Only through specificity and clarity combined with the freedom to explore can a piece of theater tell a sincere and compelling story.
