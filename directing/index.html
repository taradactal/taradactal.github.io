Acting Resume

I first started directing in college as a means to create the actor driven work that I wanted to be a part of.

I believe that our primary goal as artists is to story tell. To that end I love creating collaborative rehearsal environments which provide common ground for innovation, creativity, and discovery. I use viewpoints, clowning, and Linklater techniques to empower actors to create a language that translates instincts into action in the rehearsal room.
