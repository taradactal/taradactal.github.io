Acting Resume

Born and raised in Queens New York, I first discovered performing at my local dance studio. I studied ballet, tap, and modern dance for twelve before I attended Frank Sinara School of the Arts for high school. While there I caught the acting bug and have been luck enough to not look back.

I got degrees in Theater Studies and Phsychology at Columbia University where the Queens girl in me realized that she loves the Upper West Side. During college I studied abroad at the British American Drama Accademy and collaborated with various NY based theater companies based in NY.

Maybe one more bit where I say something interesting and funny. But that's all I know to say for now.
