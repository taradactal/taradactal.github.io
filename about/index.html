New York Based, AEA Actor

Tara has most notably appeared in The Mysteries at the Flea Theater where she played a series of increasingly distressed biblical women, and Kapow-i Gogo at the People’s Inprov Theater where she wore antlers and puppeteered cardboard cutouts with the gusto of an anime character.

Tara currently trains at the UCB, Kimball Studio, and with Blessed Unrest - a company that specializes in international collaboration and devised physical theater.

In her free time Tara tinkers with web design, is aggressively snuggly with her cat, and is fighting the uphill battle of “becoming a runner”. Also, one time, Billy Porter held her face and told her she was funny -she's never letting that go.
